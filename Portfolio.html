About Me

Irtwange Emmy Terwase is a dedicated professional with a strong foundation in product management and a background in Agricultural and Environmental Engineering. Born on November 27, 1994, in Makurdi, Benue State, Nigeria, Emmy has consistently demonstrated a commitment to excellence in product development and management.


Emmy is currently pursuing a Master's of Sciences in Post-Harvest Engineering and Technology at the Center for Food Technology and Research Makurdi, respectively. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Agricultural and Environmental Engineering from the Federal University of Agriculture, Makurdi, where he graduated with a Second Class Upper Division in March 2019.


Emmy's career is marked by his expertise in product management, where he has successfully developed and executed product strategies, managed product lifecycles, and conducted market research. His experience spans various roles, including:

  • Allroute Logistics: As the Product Manager for a logistics service company in Lagos, Emmy has been instrumental in developing product strategies, optimizing service delivery processes, and ensuring customer satisfaction.
  • Chawbox: Emmy co-founded this e-commerce website, where he played a pivotal role in product development, market research, and customer engagement strategies.
  • Mealtrips: Serving as the Business Development Manager for this online food delivery business, Emmy successfully drove business growth through strategic partnerships, market analysis, and product optimization.
  • HNG Internship 11: During his internship at HNG, Emmy played a significant role in the development of a Remote Bingo game for platforms like Slack and Zoom. He was responsible for creating the product requirement document, functional requirement document, and developing frontend and backend tickets on Linear. Emmy also created GitHub issues and collaborated closely with the development team to ensure the project's success.
